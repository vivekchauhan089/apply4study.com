# Hybrid Supply Chain Model - Complete Process Flow

## End-to-End Process Architecture

### 1. **Process Initiation**
- **RFQ Creation**: Buyers create detailed procurement requests
- **Industry Selection**: Automatic routing based on product category
- **Supplier Discovery**: AI-powered matching with qualified suppliers
- **Multi-tier Sourcing**: Primary, secondary, and backup suppliers

### 2. **Procurement Workflow**
```
RFQ ‚Üí Supplier Selection ‚Üí Negotiation ‚Üí Contract ‚Üí PO Creation ‚Üí Delivery ‚Üí Payment
```

**Stages:**
- **Stage 1**: RFQ Creation & Publishing
- **Stage 2**: Supplier Response & Evaluation  
- **Stage 3**: Contract Negotiation & Award
- **Stage 4**: Purchase Order Generation
- **Stage 5**: Production/Sourcing
- **Stage 6**: Quality Control & Inspection
- **Stage 7**: Logistics & Delivery
- **Stage 8**: Invoice Processing & Payment

### 3. **Manufacturing Integration**
```
Material Receipt ‚Üí Production Planning ‚Üí Manufacturing ‚Üí Quality Assurance ‚Üí Packaging ‚Üí Shipping
```

**Key Features:**
- **BOM Management**: Bill of materials tracking
- **Capacity Planning**: Resource allocation optimization
- **Production Scheduling**: Just-in-time manufacturing
- **Quality Gates**: Multi-stage quality checkpoints

### 4. **Distribution Network**
```
Warehouse ‚Üí Regional Hubs ‚Üí Local Distribution ‚Üí Last Mile Delivery
```

**Multi-Modal Logistics:**
- **Standard Shipping**: Regular products
- **Cold Chain**: Temperature-sensitive items
- **Hazmat Handling**: Chemical/pharmaceutical products
- **Express Delivery**: Urgent requirements
